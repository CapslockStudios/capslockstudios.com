About the Game

My Light and Spirit

My Light and Spirit is a grid-based puzzle game where you use light to shape the world around you. Guide beams across intricate grids to make shadow blocks vanish, reflect light off mirrors, uncover new paths, and much more.

With over 100 handcrafted levels, My Light and Spirit challenges players to think creatively and master the interplay between light and shadow.
